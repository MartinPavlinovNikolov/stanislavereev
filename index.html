About SMP

Scalp Micropigmentation is safe, non-surgical and non-invasive treatment, which is affordable solution for restoring confidence in both male and female individuals who suffer from hair loss.

Micro pigmentation involves no down time and there is very little or no pain felt during the treatment. It is possible the appearance of a mild skin redness, which however should settle within a few hours following the treatment.

We offer free of charge initial consultation in order to gain an understanding of what is the desired result from the treatment. The patients will be guided through the process with professional advice to match their expectations.

The treatment typically requires three sessions. Each session lasts between 1 to 4 hours, depending on the area treated.

SMP can be a great option in dealig with any sort of scars, including those from previous hair transplantation surgery.
